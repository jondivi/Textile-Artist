Sarah Divi creates beautiful hand-knit artwork that blends the comforting feel of a beloved sweater with the vibrant energy of graffiti.
Her unique fiber artwork ranges from small living room accents to large chain-link fences to transforming entire rooms into captivating, immersive experiences.

Sarah discovered her love for fiber art while knitting her college thesis from unraveled sweaters. From that moment on, she has turned unwanted clothing and other unexpected materials into stunning site-specific installations, using innovative materials in unique ways. Her creations are a beautiful combination of traditional crafts and modern art, crafted on a grand scale.

Sarah holds a BFA degree from the Purchase College School of Art+Design and lives in Westchester, New York. She has also completed various art fellowships and teaching residencies, including with ArtsWestchester, Hudson River Museum, and WUJS Arad.
